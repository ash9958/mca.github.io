Department of Computer Applications

Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

  1. To develop software professionals according to industrial requirements using modern software tools.

  2. To inculcate the power of team-building abilities, problem solving capabilities,entrepreneurial skills in the students.

  3. To make learners industry ready by nurturing their communication and technical skills which can be further applied in social and ethical context.


Program Specific Outcomes (PSO)

  1. Students will be able to apply logical and software development skills in areas like database design, client-server solution development, application programming and financial applications.

  2. Students will have the capabilities to analyse, design, test and produce optimized or accurate solutions to problems using acquired knowledge in various domains of mathematics and computer applications so as to contribute to the needs of industry and society as an individual as well as a team member.
